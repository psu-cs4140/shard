# The Vampire's Manor

## Mission:
    - Rescue the mayor from the evil Vampire!

## Map layout:
    - 26 rooms consisting of the starting area (outside the manor),
a small sewer with a few monsters, and inside the manor where the player
will face various obstacles to their goal.

## How to Beat:

### Gain entry to the manor:
    - Vampire's Thrall has a quest, will provide you a key to the manor
    in exchange for completing quest (find and talk to thrall)
    - Quest takes you into the sewer where you must hunt an ooze 
    creature that stole the thrall's favorite slippers (hunt the ooze,
    obtain the slippers)
    - Once the slippers are obtained, return them to the thrall to 
    receive the Manor Key. (Give slippers to thrall, obtain key)
    - Use the key on the manor.

### Locate the Mayor:
    - The Mayor will be guarded by the Vampire. The Vampire will be too strong
    to fight at first.
    - Obtain Holy Incantation. When used on the Vampire, it will become 
    vulnerable to attacks. (Find holy incantation spell, hidden behind a 
    locked door with a riddle. The key is inside the jaws of a statue. Riddle 
    is solved by saying correct phrase to the statue)
    - Obtain silver sword. Must battle possessed armor that holds the sword. 
    (Find possessed armor, defeat it, pick up and equip silver sword)

### Fight the Vampire:
    - Once you have done the necessary steps to be able to damage the Vampire,
    you must battle him once and for all. (Find the vampire, begin fight
    by using the holy incantation and attacking with the silver sword)
